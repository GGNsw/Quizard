Welcome to Quizard

Quizard intends to make watching movies an even more enjoyable experience. Nowadays, we expose ourselves to an endless flow of information, with the pursuit of taking our minds off reality. This way, it is not uncommon to use our phones or computers while watching television, so we don't feel bored for not even one second. But this behavior does not allow people to pay attention, enjoy the movie or feel a connection with the story it's been told.

This project seeks to avoid that. Quizard encourages users to pay attention to movies so they can be “rewarded” with a good mark on the quiz, allowing users to fully enjoy the movies they are watching.
